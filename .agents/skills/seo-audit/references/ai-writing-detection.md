## Em Dashes: The Primary AI Tell
**The em dash (—) has become one of the most reliable markers of AI-generated content.**

### Guidelines
- Use commas for most parenthetical information
- Use colons to introduce explanations or lists
- Use parentheses for supplementary information
- Reserve em dashes for rare, deliberate emphasis only

## Overused Verbs
| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| delve (into) | explore, examine, investigate |
| leverage | use, apply |
| optimise | improve, refine |
| utilise | use |
| facilitate | help, enable |
| foster | encourage, develop |
| streamline | simplify |
| enhance | improve |

---

## Overused Adjectives
| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| robust | strong, reliable |
| comprehensive | complete, thorough |
| pivotal | key, critical |
| innovative | new, original |
| seamless | smooth, easy |

## Phrases That Signal AI Writing
- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "In today's digital age..."
- "That being said..."
- "It's worth mentioning that..."
- "In conclusion..."
- "Whether you're a [X], [Y], or [Z]..."

---

## How to Self-Check
1. Read your text aloud.
2. Ask: "Would I say this in a conversation?"
3. Check for repetitive sentence structures.
4. Verify each intensifier adds genuine meaning.
